# Customer Journey Map  
## Body Image Issues Among College Students (18–21)

### Journey Stages

| Stage        | Action | Thoughts | Feelings | Problems |
|-------------|--------|----------|----------|----------|
| Awareness   | Notices appearance (weight, skin, body) | "Do I look okay?" | Insecure | Influenced by social media |
| Comparison  | Compares with others online/offline | "They look better than me" | Anxious | Unrealistic standards |
| Friction    | Avoids photos, overthinks looks | "People will judge me" | Stressed | Low confidence |
| Attempt to Fix | Dieting, gym, filters | "I need to improve" | Hopeful | Pressure, inconsistency |
| Outcome     | Self-acceptance OR continued comparison | "I’m okay" / "Not enough" | Confident / Unhappy | Mental stress |

---

### Summary
This journey map shows how college students experience body image issues, starting from awareness and comparison to emotional struggles and final outcomes.
